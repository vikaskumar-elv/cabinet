About Width
Width

This measurement refers to the overall external width of the cabinet carcass . Measured from outside to outside. The thickness of both side carcass structure (16 mm each) is already included in this dimension. As a result, the internal usable width inside your cabinet will be 32 mm less than the external width. Please note: The final assembled cabinet width maybe up to 1-2 mm larger due to minor tolerances. We hieghly recommend allowing this clearance in your overal space planning to ensure proper fit.

Product Details

Product Description:

Add versatile right-opening storage to your kitchen with this Base Single Right Door Custom Cabinet. Designed to accommodate your choice of handles, this cabinet provides flexible storage that integrates perfectly with your kitchen layout.

This premium Custom Base Cabinet is proudly Flatpack manufactured in our Melbourne factory using high-quality Australian-made materials. Featuring single right-opening door with adjustable internal shelf, this cabinet delivers reliable storage for kitchens, laundry designs, and utility spaces.

Single right door provides convenient access. Adjustable shelf customises internal storage. Made-to-order sizing ensures perfect fit.

FAQ:

What Are The Size Options For This Product?

This product is fully customisable through our online configurator. You can set the width from 150mm to 600mm, height from 400mm to 1200mm, and depth from 90mm to 700mm. All dimensions can be adjusted in 1mm increments for a precise fit in your space.

What Materials Are Available?

Select separate materials for carcass and doors. For carcass, choose between White Melamine Chipboard or Colour Melamine Chipboard. For doors, options include White Melamine Chipboard, Budget Melamine, Premium Melamine, Polytec Colour range, or 2PAC Range for a painted finish. Use the dropdown menus above and click Select Colour to browse finishes and preview your selection.

What Hinge Type And Quantity Should I Use?

Hinge quantity depends on door height: 2 hinges for doors up to 1000mm, 3 hinges for 1000-1500mm, 4 hinges for doors over 1500mm. Blum soft-close hinges are included when you order doors with your cabinet.
