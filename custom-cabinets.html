Why Choose Cabinetworx Custom Flat Pack Cabinets?

At Cabinetworx, we’ve re-imagined how Australians design and order cabinetry. Instead of choosing from limited, pre-set sizes, our platform gives you full control over the exact dimensions, layout and configuration you need. Every cabinet is made to order in our Melbourne factory using premium Australian materials and precision CNC machining, ensuring a perfect fit for your space every time.

Whether you’re renovating a kitchen, building a laundry, upgrading a wardrobe, or fitting out a new home, our custom flatpack system makes professional-grade cabinetry accessible, affordable, and easy to install.



How Our Custom Flatpack Process Works

Designing and ordering your custom cabinets online is simple:


1. Choose your cabinet type

Select from base cabinets, overhead cabinets, tall cabinets, drawers, corner solutions, wardrobe units, and more.

Every product includes all the structural components, pre-drilled panels, and hardware ready for assembly.


2. Enter your custom measurements

Unlike fixed-size modular systems, Cabinetworx allows millimetre-accurate customisation.

You decide the width, height, and depth — ensuring a seamless fit even in tricky or uneven spaces.


3. Select your door style and finishes

Choose your preferred material, finish, and door style from our curated collection. All options are Australian-made and selected for durability, clean edges, and long-term performance.


4. Order online & we manufacture locally

Once your order is confirmed, our Melbourne factory produces your custom cabinets with industry-leading accuracy. Flatpacks are then packaged safely and can be collected or shipped to your chosen depot.

FAQs About Custom Flat Pack Cabinets

Are custom cabinets more expensive than modular?

Not necessarily. Cabinetworx allows you to customise sizes without a premium joinery cost. Pricing updates instantly as you design, giving full transparency.


Can I install these cabinets myself?

Yes. All panels are pre-drilled and designed for easy DIY assembly. Installers and builders also love the accuracy of our system.


Do you supply doors and drawer fronts?

Yes — all cabinetry can be ordered with or without doors and fronts. Simply tick “Exclude from my order” if you prefer to use your own.


Are the cabinets suitable for all areas of the home?

Absolutely — kitchens, laundries, wardrobes, offices, showrooms and more.
