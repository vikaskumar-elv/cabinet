About Us

Our Company Story

At Cabinetworx, we believe quality cabinetry should be accessible, customisable, and proudly Australian-made. For over 15 years, we've been helping homeowners, builders, and renovators bring their vision to life with precision-cut, fully customisable flatpack cabinets. From kitchens and laundries to wardrobes and storage spaces, our designs combine craftsmanship, technology, and style, all delivered ready for easy DIY assembly. Our mission is simple: to make professional-quality cabinetry effortless and affordable for every home.

We are Australian Based & Design!
